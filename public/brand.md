# Brand

Key elements of the Yash Timbadiya visual identity, including the mark, logotype, clear space, and color. Right-click any logo on the page to copy it as SVG or download the full brand assets pack.

## Mark

The mark is the primary symbol of the identity. Keep it monochrome and never recolor, rotate, or distort it.

Download: /assets/yash14-brand/yash14-mark.svg

## Logotype

The logotype is the wordmark version of the brand. Use it where the full name needs to read clearly.

Download: /assets/yash14-brand/yash14-logotype.svg

## Clear Space

Maintain a clear space of 1/2 the height of the Mark on all sides of the logo, except in limited cases where the logo remains clearly recognizable and legible.

## Color

The identity is monochrome. Use Ink on light surfaces and Paper on dark surfaces to keep the mark legible.

- Ink — #09090B
- Paper — #FFFFFF
